Assignments

The majority of our assignments were papers regarding our readings and reflections on our class discussions.

My personal favorite project was an individual presentation where we were tasked with teach the class about anything internet issue that related to the readings. My topic was about privacy and data invasion, so I decided to present about the security of DNA companies like 23andMe and Ancestry.com. This presentation allowed me to do amazing research about the dangers and innacuracy of DNA companies.
