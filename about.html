About the Course

Our class topics could range anywhere from “Is the internet making us dumb?” to “Fake News!” We had daily discussions regarding scholarly readings and cautionary tales for each week. The class challenged pretty much every preconceived idea I had about the digital world and I can confidently say my horizons were broadened after each class meeting. While its name sounds extremely pessimistic, “The Internet is a Trash Fire” gave me hope that generations of digital communities would end up ok.
